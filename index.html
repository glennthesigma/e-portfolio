SMO Open 2024

Award: Honourable Mention

On May 29 2024, I went for my first Singaporean Mathematics Olympiad (SMO) Open. I have only participated in SMO once in 2023 (Senior Category), and I could only solve 2 questions. This made me lose confidence in my ability. However, that setback pushed me to learn an array of mathematical content during my free time. This allowed me to be more familiar with content out of my O-level and A-level syllabuses. With the help of a 1-month SMO training session and my due diligence, I managed to clinch an Honourable Mention title to my name.

AMC Senior 2024

Award: Distinction

After the SMO results were announced, I discovered another competition — the Australian Mathematics Competition (AMC) — and eagerly seized the opportunity. Initially, I assumed my SMO experience would give me an edge, but the AMC emphasized accuracy, speed, and attention to question phrasing. This frustrated me during practice, as poor time management and overlooking details cost me marks. Without school training sessions, I relied on myself and my peers. Through persistent practice, I improved my focus and efficiency, enabling me to perform well on the competition day and earn a Distinction award.
